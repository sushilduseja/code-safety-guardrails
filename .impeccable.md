## Design Context

### Users
This project is primarily viewed by engineering hiring managers, recruiters, and technical peers as part of a GitHub portfolio. The main job is to quickly demonstrate that the product is real, coherent, and built with sound engineering judgment.

### Brand Personality
Professional, credible, polished.
The interface should feel visually appealing and intentional without looking over-designed or theatrical.

### Aesthetic Direction
MVP polish for a prototype. Use a clean, professional visual language with a light theme and restrained accents. Avoid purple-forward palettes and avoid generic AI-demo aesthetics.

### Design Principles
1. Make the engineering value legible in under 30 seconds.
2. Prefer clarity and polish over visual novelty.
3. Use restrained accent color, strong spacing, and clear hierarchy.
4. Every interaction state should feel deliberate and reliable.
5. Avoid trendy AI-demo cues that make the work feel generic.
